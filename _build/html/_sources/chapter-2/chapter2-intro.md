The *economical expansion problem* (EEP) for
$(A,B)$ is to find a semi-positive $n$-vector $p>0$
and a number $\beta\in\mathbb{R}$, such that

$$
&\min_{\beta} \hspace{2mm} \beta \\
&\text{s.t. }\hspace{2mm}Bp \leq \beta Ap
$$
